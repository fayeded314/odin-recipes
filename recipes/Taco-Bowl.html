Healthy Protein Taco Taco-Bowl

Cheesy Taco Bowl with Tons of Protein and Fiber

Ingredients

  • Can of Black Beans
  • Pico De Gallo
  • Chicken
  • Tomatoes
  • Cilantro
  • Rice

Recipe Order

  1. Cook chicken till fully cooked and white inside
  2. At the same time prep the rice to cook by boiliing water and adding rice, stir not so often, and rice will be finished when grain is soft and chewable
  3. Warm up can of beans in a pot on a stove
  4. Cut the cilantro and dice the tomatoes to mix
  5. Now you can mix all the ingredients together to complete the dish!
