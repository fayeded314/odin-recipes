Shrimp Alfredo

The Alfredo of the Sea

Ingredients

  • Pasta noodles
  • Shrimp
  • Garlic Salt
  • Soy Sauce
  • Butter
  • Canned Alfredo Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Water
  • Cheese

Recipe Instructions

  1. Peel off the shrimp tails and toss into a hot pan with butter
  2. Meanwhile, prep a pot with water, the pasta noodles, and then salt the pot
  3. Add 1 tbsp of Soysauce to the Shrimp and season with garlic salt lower hear and let simmer
  4. Add alfredo sauce to a hot skillet mix in a slice of cheese and pepper and stir till consistent
  5. Once all ingredients are prepared mix together and garnish to your optional liking
