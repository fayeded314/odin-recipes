Avocado Turkey-Burgers

A Tasty Lean Protein Filled Turkey Burger

Ingredients

  • Turkey Patties
  • Hamburger Bun
  • Tomatoe
  • Lettuce
  • Avocado
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Butter
  • Mayo
  • Cheese

Recipe Instruction

  1. Place your turkey patties on a hot skillet with some butter
  2. Prep bun by toasting on another pan with butter till golden
  3. Prep avocado by cutting then smashing into a paste form
  4. Cut tomatoes
  5. Salt and pepper tomatoes
  6. Spread mayo on golden toasted buns
  7. assemble from bottom to top burger first, then avocado paste, which you can top with cheese, and finally add tomatoes then lettuce.
  8. Add the top bun then cut to give a beautiful intersection
